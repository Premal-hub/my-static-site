FAQs

What should I expect during my first visit?

During your first visit, we will perform a comprehensive eye exam, which includes checking your vision, assessing your eye health, and discussing any concerns you may have.

How often should I have my eyes checked?

We recommend an eye exam every one to two years, but this may vary based on your specific health needs and risk factors.
